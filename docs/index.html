It takes Two

Software Engineering Project (1DL650) · Group 5 jiawen.chen.3875@student.uu.se

We are It Takes Two, a team passionate about creating meaningful and human-centered software experiences. We are developing AI Emotional Journal, a cross-platform journaling app that helps people explore and reflect on their emotions through daily writing. Our goal is to combine natural language processing with sentiment analysis to interpret users’ emotions, offer warm and empathetic responses, and visualize emotional patterns over time.

Through this project, we hope to create a calm and human-like space where users can better understand their inner world and cultivate emotional balance.
