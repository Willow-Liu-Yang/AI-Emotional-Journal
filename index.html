Group 5 | AI Emotional Journal Project

Project Goals

AI Emotional Journal is a cross-platform journaling application designed to help users explore, understand, and reflect on their emotions through everyday writing.

By combining Natural Language Processing (NLP) and sentiment analysis, it interprets the emotional tone behind each entry, offers empathetic feedback, and visualizes emotional patterns over time.

The project aims to promote emotional awareness and mental well-being through a calm, human-centered digital experience.
